Brainfog and Daydreaming
Texto

Sometimes I feel as if daydreaming and brainfog were the same thing. I see it like this: My life on these moments is filled with responsabilities, of which I do not enjoy everyone. But daydreaming has always been present, as if it was a reminder of the future of life. But I do not have the luxury of embracing day-dreaming enough. This is wehere brain fog comes in. Brain fog is the mist of day-dreaming, trying to claim its place on my brain. It is also like excitement. For me, excitement is a poison. It is not safe for me. When I get to excited, and when the idea of something, make me supper happy it is almost as if I couldn't control it. It overpowers me. I do not understand why I have this super intense emotions. Frustration is not only frustration, but desperation and anger. Happinness is not only happinness, but painful excitement. Sadness is not only sadness, but low depressing moods. I really want to help myself. I really do. I want to be consistent, I want to be purposeful and determined. I want to experience the normal mode of human life.

I do not have many taboo subjects that I am eager to disclose, but one. Ever since being diagnose with a deficit of attention. My body and my mind have become interesting to me, the way it aches and shifts on place. I can suddenly feel the river of anxierty through my body. And the waterfall of guilt descending to my stomach. My legs can't stop moving, flapping like wings. Every chair is uncomfortable and every concept boring. This is when I remember, I haven't take my ADHD medicine. But I don't believe it, there must be more to this. There must be more to this feeling that I feel. Because if the reason is, that all humans experience such tiring experiences. How come everyone but me manage it normally. How must I handle it? Another subject of major interest to me, is, the easiness in which people trascend from one task to another, consciously, flowing from the instructions and setting their mind to work. No body should be this articulated, how? My differences from this mass and myself, have become more and more aparent. And nothing has consoled me. If I am different, how am i supposed to succeed?
